Run Facebook Ad Campaigns

Businesses advertise on Facebook to reach more customers online. These steps will help you get started on running a Facebook ad campaign.

How to Run Ad Campaigns on Facebook

1

Choose your ad objective

To choose the right ad objective, answer the question: "What's the most important outcome I want from this ad?" Options include driving traffic, increasing conversions, or boosting local awareness.

2

Understand your audience

Who do you want to reach? Find people based on age, gender, location, interests, behaviors or who they follow. Highly targeted ads create a better return on investment.

3

Decide where to advertise

Next, choose where you want to run your ad. Whether that's on Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, Audience Network, or across them all, you can select specific placements within each app.

4

Place your order

Once you set your daily or lifetime ad budget and time period, you submit your ad. It goes to Facebook's automated ad auction which helps get your ad to the right people.

5

Measure and learn from results

When your ad is running, you can track performance in Ads Manager. See how many people you reached and gauge the effectiveness of the visual format you selected.
