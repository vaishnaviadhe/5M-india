Voucher & Discount Campaigns

```

Voucher campaigns are one of the most effective promotional strategies, offering discounts through unique or shared promo codes to drive conversions and engagement.

Types of Voucher Campaigns

  • Bulk unique codes for individual customers
  • Standalone public promo codes
  • Cart-level automatic discounts

Benefits

  • Boost sales instantly
  • Attract new customers
  • Encourage repeat purchases
```
